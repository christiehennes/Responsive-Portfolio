About Me

Hello! Welcome to my portfolio.

I'm a San Diego native with a passion for technology and grew up traveling and exploring the mountains of Southern California. From a young age, I was always interested in business, entrepreneurship, and problem solving. I graduated from the University of Southern California with a degree in Business and Computer Science.


Some of my passions include building impactful products, helping others achieve their goals, and using technology to solve non-technical problems. I currenly head up product at a local event ticketing software company, TicketSauce. I am responsible for directing and building our product roadmap, gathering and defining requirements, and overseeing the end-to-end delivery of all features.


My goal is to design and devlop digital products to improve the quality of everyday life while solving challenging and meaningful problems. Take a peek into my portfolio to see what I've been up to!
